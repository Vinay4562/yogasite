Nutrition's

Our nutrition class at Yogga focuses on teaching you about the importance of healthy eating and well-being. Throughout the course, we cover various topics, including:

  • The basic principles of nutrition
  • Macronutrients (carbohydrates, proteins, and fats)
  • Micronutrients (vitamins and minerals)
  • Balanced diets and dietary guidelines
  • Meal planning and preparation
  • Nutrition and wellness

Attending this class will provide you with valuable knowledge and practical skills to help you maintain a healthy lifestyle. By the end of the course, you will have a better understanding of how your diet impacts your overall health and well-being.
